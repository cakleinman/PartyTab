# PartyTab Product Hunt Launch

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### 2 Weeks Before
- [ ] Create Product Hunt account (if not existing)
- [ ] Build "Coming Soon" page to collect followers
- [ ] Reach out to potential hunters (people with 500+ followers)
- [ ] Prepare all assets (see below)

### 1 Week Before
- [ ] Finalize launch date (Tuesday-Thursday recommended)
- [ ] Confirm hunter (or self-hunt)
- [ ] Schedule social media announcements
- [ ] Prep email to early users asking for support

### Launch Day
- [ ] Post at 12:01 AM PST (Product Hunt resets daily)
- [ ] Share on all social channels
- [ ] Respond to EVERY comment within 30 minutes
- [ ] Keep updating first comment with FAQs

## Product Hunt Listing Content

### Tagline (60 characters max)
```
Split group expenses without the spreadsheet drama
```

**Alternatives:**
- "The easiest way to split trip costs with friends"
- "Stop chasing payments after group trips"
- "Group expense tracking that actually makes sense"

### Description (260 characters max)
```
PartyTab makes splitting group expenses dead simple. Add expenses as you go - who paid, how much, who was there. We calculate the minimum payments needed to settle up. No more spreadsheets, no more awkward Venmo requests. Free to use, no app download required.
```
