## Production Notes

### Equipment Needed
- Phone with good camera (iPhone 12+ ideal)
- Ring light or good natural lighting
- Simple background (solid wall, or lifestyle setting)

### Editing Tips
- Use CapCut for editing
- Add captions (85% of TikTok is watched on mute)
- Fast cuts keep attention
- Use trending sounds when relevant

### Best Posting Times (EST)
- 7-9 AM (morning scroll)
- 12-1 PM (lunch break)
- 7-10 PM (evening wind-down)
