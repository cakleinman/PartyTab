Non-permissive licenses to track
--------------------------------
MPL-2.0 (weak copyleft, file-level):
  - @vercel/analytics
  - axe-core
  - lightningcss (+ platform binaries)
  - web-push

LGPL-3.0-or-later (weak copyleft, dynamic linking allowed):
  - @img/sharp-libvips-{darwin-arm64,linux-arm64,linuxmusl-arm64}
    (transitive via sharp; pre-built native binaries, dynamically loaded)

Both license families are acceptable for a closed-source SaaS as long as we do
not modify and redistribute the libraries themselves. No remediation required.

CI gate
-------
.github/workflows/ci.yml runs license-checker with --onlyAllow against the
allowlist below. If a new license appears, the build fails — investigate the
new package, decide whether it's acceptable, and either drop the dep or add
the license here (and to the CI allowlist).

Allowlist:
  MIT, ISC, Apache-2.0, BSD-2-Clause, BSD-3-Clause, CC0-1.0, CC-BY-4.0,
  0BSD, Unlicense, Python-2.0, LGPL-3.0-or-later, MPL-2.0
